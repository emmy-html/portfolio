A creative touch

Due to my background in art and design, I can give your website that creative touch that will make it stand out from the rest! Whether you want something colorful and quirky, clean and modern, or somewhere in between, I know how to bring your vision to life and make it uniquely you.

Responsive design

In today’s day and age, you never know where your customers are viewing your website! Whether they’re visiting from their iPhone, tablet, desktop, or something in between, I’ll ensure that your website looks (and functions) beautifully no matter what device it’s being viewed on.

E-Commerce for everyone

Whether you want to start selling products on your existing website, or need a shop refresh, I’ve got your needs covered! I have experience with website builders such as Squarespace, as well as integration with platforms like Woocommerce on WordPress. We can work together to make sure your shopping cart is always full!

WordPress Customization & Integration

I can provide WordPress services that allow you to really utilize all that the platform has to offer. If you’re transferring over from another blogging platform, you can still keep your data while upgrading to a better look and easier platform!
