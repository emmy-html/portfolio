about emery jordan

I’m a front end developer with a designer’s eye. My background in art and design allows me to see the beauty in just about anything - including all things technology. In college, I took an intro to web development class and got bit by the CSS bug! After graduating with my Associates of Applied Sciences in Digital Art & Design, I decided to take my creative skills to the next level by becoming a front-end web developer. I take pride in my eye for cohesive color schemes and clean designs for any project that comes my way. When I’m not coding, I love cooking, queer media and trying not to die in the mines of Stardew Valley.

I am interested in coding responsive designs for customer-focused services such as entertainment apps & venues, restaurants, and online retail. I would especially love to work on projects that focus on diversity & inclusion of minority & LGBTQIA+ individuals in the media and service industries. As a marginalized individual, I understand the needs of under represented groups, and my goal is to help provide a voice for those people.
